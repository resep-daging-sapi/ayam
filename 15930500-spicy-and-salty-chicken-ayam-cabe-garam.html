Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Spicy and Salty Chicken (Ayam Cabe Garam):

  1. For Fried Chicken
  2. For Spicy and Salty
  3. 1 tablespoon brown sugar (1 sendok makan gula merah)
  4. 1 teaspoon mushroom seasoning powder (1 sendok teh bubuk kaldu jamur)
  5. 1/2 teaspoon salt (1/2 sendok teh garam)
  6. 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper (1/2 sendok teh bubuk lada putih)
  7. 1 tablespoon soy sauce (1 sendok makan kecap asin)
  8. 2 scallions, chopped (2 batang daun bawang, potong-potong)
  9. 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced (6 siung bawang putih, iris halus)
  10. 6 cloves shallots, thinly sliced (6 siung bawang merah, iris halus)
  11. 10 red Thai chili, thinly chopped (10 cabe rawit, potong-potong)

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Spicy and Salty Chicken (Ayam Cabe Garam)

1
English language: Marinade and fry the chicken
2
Heat a small sauce pan on a medium heat, pour 3 table spoon of olive oil. Stir fry shallots and garlic until crispy. Drain the excess oil, put aside.
3
Transfer the remain oil from the small sauce pan into a medium frying pan, saut√© the chili, then add the scallion, stir in brown sugar, mushroom seasoning powder, salt, ground white pepper and soy sauce. Stir until combined.
4
Add in the fried chicken. Stir until the chicken fully coated. Then add in the mixture crispy garlic and shallots. Spread with some cashews (optional
5
Enjoy with hot steamed rice üòã
6
Indonesian language: Marinasi dan goreng ayam.
7
Panaskan penggorengan, tuang 3 sendok makan minyak goreng, goreng bawang merah dan bawang putih sampai krispy, tiris dan sisihkan.
8
Pindahkan sisa minyak menggoreng bawang putih dan bawang merah ke penggorengan yang lebih besar, kemudian tumis cabai rawit sampai layu, masukkan daun bawang, kemudian masukkan gula merah, bubuk kaldu jamur, garam, bubuk lada putih dan kecap asin, aduk sampai tercampur rata.
9
Masukkan ayam goreng, aduk sampai bumbu meresap ke ayam, kemudian masukkan bawang merah dan bawang putih goreng, aduk kembali. Tabur kacang mete, sesuai selera.
10

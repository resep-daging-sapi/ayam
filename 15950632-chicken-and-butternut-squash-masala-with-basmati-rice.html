Chicken and Butternut Squash Masala with Basmati Rice

Dipos pada November 27, 2021

Anda bisa membuat Chicken and Butternut Squash Masala with Basmati Rice memakai 12 jenis bahan dan 7 langkah pembuatan. Berikut ini cara dalam membuat hidangannya.

A combination of garam masala, ginger and garlic brought depth to tomato sauce and tender meat. You can make it at home. No more take out dishes.

Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Chicken and Butternut Squash Masala with Basmati Rice:

  1. 2 chicken breast, skinless, boneless, and cut into 1 inch dice (2 dada ayam, potong dadu 1 inch)
  2. 1 teaspoon salt (1 sendok teh garam)
  3. 1 teaspoon ground black pepper (1 sendok teh bubuk lada hitam)
  4. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (2 sendok makan minyak goreng)
  5. 3 cups butternut squash, 1/2 inch dice (3 cups labu mentega, potong dadu 1/2 inch)
  6. 1 onion, diced (1 bawang bombay, potong dadu)
  7. 2 teaspoons ginger, minced (2 sendok teh jahe, rajang halus)
  8. 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped (3 siung bawang putih, rajang halus)
  9. 3 cups tomato puree (3 cup pasta tomat)
  10. 1/2 cup heavy cream (1/2 cup krim)
  11. as needed Fresh cilantro((daun ketumbar segar)
  12. Basmati Rice

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Chicken and Butternut Squash Masala with Basmati Rice

1
In a small bowl, combine garam masala, salt and pepper. (Dalam wadah kecil, campur garam masala, garam dan bubuk lada hitam)
2
In a medium bowl, bring together 1/2 of the spices mixture with chicken pieces, combine and put aside. (Dalam wadah ukuran sedang, campur ayam dan 1/2 campuran bumbu di atas. Sisihkan)
3
In a big sauce pan over a medium heat, pour 2 tablespoons vegetable oil. Then stir fry garlic, ginger, onion until fragrant, then add in the remaining spices and cook until fragrant.
4
Stir in chicken and tomato pur√©e, bring to simmer, and cook until chicken is cooked through and butternut squash is tender for 10-12 minutes. (Masukkan ayam dan pasta tomat, biarkan mendidih dan sampai ayam matang dan labu mentega empuk, kurang lebih selama 10-12 menit)
5
Reduce heat to low, stir in heavy cream, mix well. Seasoning with salt and pepper to taste. (Ubah kompor ke api paling kecil, masukkan krim, aduk.)
6
Sprinkle with cilantro. (Tabur dengan daun ketumbar segar)
7
Enjoy with basmati rice.

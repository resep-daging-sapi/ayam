Tried the recipe from Chef Devina Hermawan using Oven. Simple and easy

Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Sate Ayam Khas Senayan with Oven:

  1. 400 grams of Chicken Thigh or Leg
  2. For Marinade
  3. 1 tbsp chicken powder
  4. 1 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  5. 1/2 tsp pepper
  6. 4 tbsp peanut butter
  7. 1 tbsp thick coconut milk
  8. 2 pc shallot
  9. 1 clove garlic
  10. Peanut Sauce
  11. 3 tbsp peanut butter
  12. 1 tbsp fried shallots
  13. 1/2 tbsp fried garlic
  14. 1/2 tsp salt
  15. 200 ml water
  16. 1 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  17. 1/2 tsp flavour enhancer

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Sate Ayam Khas Senayan with Oven

1
Slice the chicken lengthwise
2
Marinate the chicken with all ingredients above for 2 hours minimum in fridge
3
For the peanut sauce, mix all the peanut sauce ingredients and cook and stir until thickens
4
Once you have done the marinade, put all the chicken slice into the bamboo stick as a chicken satay. Limit the slice to max 4 in a stick.
5
Pre Heat the oven with 180 degree Celsius for 10 mins. Put the Chicken Satay in the oven with 180 degree Celsius for 20-25 mins. Don‚Äôt forget to rotate the chicken satay every 5 mins.
